The Gardens of Babylon: Ancient Wonder of the World

Explore the legendary Hanging Gardens of Babylon, one of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World. Journey through the opulent palace gardens created by King Nebuchadnezzar II for his homesick wife Amytis of Media, who longed for the green mountains of her homeland.

Discover the engineering marvel of the terraced gardens rising like a green mountain in the desert, with their innovative irrigation systems drawing water from the Euphrates River. Learn about the exotic plants and trees collected from across the vast Babylonian Empire.

Experience the daily life in ancient Babylon, from the bustling markets near the Ishtar Gate to the ziggurats reaching toward the heavens. Witness the cultural achievements of this Mesopotamian civilization that gave us some of the earliest written laws, astronomical observations, and mathematical innovations.
